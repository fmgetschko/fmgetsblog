Initial post

November 27, 2016

It’s been a while that I’ve been wanting to revive my blog. One of the many things I learned at my awesome journey @DBC in 2015 is that we learn things everyday, and mostly we don’t realize it.

That’s the main reason why I restarted my blog: to write down about the things I learn, and think over about them.

I’m starting fresh, and that means that, by now, my old posts are all off. But that won’t be for long. I’ll definitely put my old learnings and meditations about many cultural aspects that surround the technology world up again, as soon as possible. It’s an essential part of my journey!

For now, I intend to weekly explore the many interesting challenges I’m facing regarding product and engineering management at an impressive fast growing startup where I happen to be.

Welcome and I hope you enjoy!

Fernanda Getschko
